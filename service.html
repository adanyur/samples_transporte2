Dedicated and consolidated

Consolidated cargo transportation

It is one in which the client does not have enough merchandise to occupy the entire space of a truck. In these cases, the client has the option of consolidating their cargo together with the cargoes of other clients, as long as they have a similar trajectory.

The main advantage is the cost of transportation. From this modality, the price of gasoline, the toll, the hiring of the driver and all other costs charged by the carrier will be divided among all the customers of said truck.

Warehousing services

Within the storage services we give different options to minimize costs.

Storage - What is storage? - It is the act of storing goods in a limited way and that these will be sold or distributed later. While a small home-based business might store its products in a spare room, basement, or garage, large businesses typically own or rent space in a building that is specially designed for storage.
Warehousing and everything that goes along with it is part of a sophisticated industry known as logistics management. Logistics includes procurement, inventory management, and distribution as other disciplines related to the product.
